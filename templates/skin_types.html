Skin Types

Dry Skin

Dry skin produces less sebum than normal skin. It can feel tight and rough, and may show visible flaking.

Oily Skin

Oily skin produces excess sebum, leading to a shiny appearance and increased likelihood of acne.

Normal Skin

Normal skin is well-balanced, neither too oily nor too dry. It has a healthy, clear appearance.

Combination Skin

Combination skin features both oily and dry areas. The T-zone (forehead, nose, and chin) is typically oilier.

Sensitive Skin

Sensitive skin reacts easily to environmental factors and skincare products, often resulting in redness or irritation.
